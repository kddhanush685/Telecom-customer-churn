# Telecom Customer Churn Analysis & Prediction 📊

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing telecom customer churn patterns and identifying the major factors that influence customer retention and customer loss.

The project includes:
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Customer churn analysis
- Churn rate calculation
- Customer segmentation
- Revenue impact analysis
- Churn probability prediction
- Interactive dashboards for business insights

The goal is to help telecom companies understand why customers leave and develop strategies to improve customer retention.

---

# Business Problem

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges in the telecom industry. Losing customers directly impacts revenue and growth.

This project answers questions like:

- What percentage of customers are leaving?
- What are the main reasons customers churn?
- Which contract types have higher churn?
- Which services impact customer retention?
- Which customers are at high risk of leaving?
- How much revenue is at risk due to churn?

---

# Dashboard Insights

## Dashboard 1: Telecom Customer Churn Overview

Key metrics:

- Total Customers: 6687
- Churned Customers: 1796
- Churn Rate: 26.9%

Main churn reasons:

- Competitor made better offer
- Competitor had better devices
- Poor support experience
- Pricing issues

Insights:

- Competitor-related factors are the biggest churn drivers.
- Customer service quality strongly affects retention.

---

## Dashboard 2: Customer Service & Revenue Analysis

Analyzed:

- Internet service usage
- Phone service
- Add-on services
- Contract type
- Payment methods
- Customer demographics

Insights:

- Month-to-month customers have higher churn probability.
- Long-term contracts show better retention.
- Customers using electronic check payments show higher churn risk.

# Key Findings

✔ Customers with month-to-month contracts have the highest churn rate.

✔ Competitor offers are the biggest reason for customer loss.

✔ Customers with higher monthly charges show increased churn probability.

✔ Payment method and service experience influence customer retention.

✔ Early identification of high-risk customers can reduce revenue loss.

---

# Business Recommendations

1. Improve customer support quality.

2. Create loyalty programs for month-to-month customers.

3. Offer competitive pricing plans.

4. Provide incentives for long-term contracts.

5. Monitor high-risk customers and provide personalized offers.
